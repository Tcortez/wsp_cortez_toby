Project Analysis

Client: Vistacomputerrepair.com

Project: Website redesign

Designer: Toby cortez

Site Intent:

The intent of Vista Computer Repair’s (VCR) website is to provide easy and quick access to helpful information and computer repair services to consumers and businesses alike. Laptop repair especially screen replacement, broken DC jacks, and virus removal seems to be the focus of the company and it is prevalent in the wording throughout the site.

The site lack focus and basic organization, there are currently 19 pages in total and an overabundance of redundancy. The site architecture has 423 w3c validation errors and it is not mobile friendly. This all results in the page being ranked low on search engines and also does not lend to a pleasurable end user experience. The site is meant to be a tool that helps the company gain exposure and revenue, as it stands the site does neither of these.

The goal of the website redesign is to restructure and reorganize the site in a way that will not only help the end user navigate it with minimal clicks, but also help improve the sites search engine ranking, and drive more traffic to the site resulting in more revenue for the company. I will streamline the content from the current 19 pages to a mere 6 pages, while improving the overall aesthetics, flow, usability, and search engine ranking. This redesign will be beneficial to both the end user and the company.
